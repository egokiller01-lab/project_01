Company Overview & Strategic Positioning

PLACHEM VINA is a B2B enterprise specializing in the design and manufacture of surface treatment chemical solutions, a core part of the global precision industry supply chain, with Bac Ninh, Vietnam as its strategic base.

Since its establishment in 2016, we have solved technical challenges in the electronics, mobile, and precision metal parts industries, solidifying our position by supplying precision chemical solutions to major global partners such as Samsung Electronics supply chain (Tier-1).
